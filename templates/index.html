Bitwise 2016

This year, we, CodeClub, IIT Kharagpur plan to maintain what we started in the previous year and stabilise our presence as one of the major college fests of the country. We will be maintaining our flagship event, BitWise and modifying the programming events from last year so that we can maintain the freshness in our problem statements and continue to befuddle the competitors. We will also be bringing back the Guest Lecture series, Quizzing, Gaming and many more on the spot events during this edition. Accomodation will be provided to external students.
